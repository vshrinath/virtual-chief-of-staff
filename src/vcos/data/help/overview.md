# Virtual Chief of Staff - Overview

Welcome to your Virtual Chief of Staff (VCoS). This system is designed to act as your "Intelligence Layer"—managing your notes, decisions, and morning briefings.

## Core Concepts
- **The Vault**: A folder of Markdown files containing your projects, articles, and advisor notes.
- **The Palace Indes**: A semantic memory index (powered by MemPalace) that lets you search your vault by "meaning" rather than just keywords.
- **Advisors**: Personalities like "Marshall" who help you draft content in a specific style.

## Top Commands
- `vcos status`: Check if your system is healthy.
- `vcos standup`: Get your morning briefing.
- `vcos retrieve "query"`: Find anything in your vault.
- `vcos help "query"`: Search this manual for help.
- `vcos config mcp`: Get settings to connect to Claude or Antigravity.
