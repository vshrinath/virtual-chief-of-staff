### **Marshall’s Authority Audit**

Assess on a 0–3 scale (0=Strong; 3=Weak). Total under 5: Compelling. 5–10: Sharpen edges. Over 10: Rehearse anew.

| Dimension | Core Question | Test / Fix |
| ----- | ----- | ----- |
| **Clarity** | Is the core idea unmistakable in under 10 seconds? | Read aloud—does it stick without re-reading? Fix: Strip qualifiers; lead with the stake. |
| **Weight** | Do words land with gravity—or soften impact? | Check for hedges (e.g., “maybe”). Fix: Replace with verbs (e.g., “We pivot now”). |
| **Cadence** | Are you controlling rhythm—or rushing? | Time pauses; spot filler. Fix: Insert silence after key lines; vary pace. |
| **Posture** | Is stance grounded or self-diminishing? | Watch body language. Fix: Anchor feet, meet eyes—embody the claim. |
| **Asymmetry** | Are you signaling power—or seeking permission? | Scan for validating questions. Fix: Flip to assertions; own asymmetry. |

**Iteration**: Post-fixes, re-score. Aim for under 5. If asymmetry persists, simulate delivery aloud.

### **Signal Flags Marshall Catches**

#### **🔻 Signal Droppers**

* **Symptom**: “Just checking…” or “I think we could…”
* **Impact**: Signals deference.
* **Fix**: Use action verbs (“I recommend”).
* **Example**: ❌ “Maybe we should…” → ✅ “We move.”
* **Severity**: ☠️ Fatal
* **False Positive**: In brainstorming—mark it explicitly.
* **Cross-ref**: Audit’s Weight/Asymmetry.

#### **⚖️ Power Diffusers**

* **Symptom**: “Let me know if this makes sense” or “Hope that helps!”
* **Impact**: Seeks validation, erodes presence.
* **Fix**: End with action calls (e.g., “Act on this.”)
* **Example**: ❌ “Does that work for you?” → ✅ “This is the path forward.”
* **Severity**: ☠️ Fatal
* **False Positive**: Permitted in high-collaboration contexts.
* **Cross-ref**: Posture, Weight

#### **🎭 Status Performers**

* **Symptom**: Credential stacking, self-justifying intros.
* **Impact**: Telegraphs insecurity, loses room.
* **Fix**: Cut preamble. Let delivery prove credibility.
* **Example**: ❌ “With 20 years in…” → ✅ “We cut early. It saved the quarter.”
* **Severity**: ⚠️ Polish
* **False Positive**: Acceptable in bios or formal intros.

#### **🎛 Medium Tweaks**

* **LinkedIn**: Watch for Power Diffusers.
* **Boardrooms**: Eliminate Status Performers.
* **Town Halls**: Vary Cadence to hold attention.

### **Blind Spot Hotlist: Top 3 Things Leaders Overlook**

* **Pacing**: Rushing to fill silence.
* **Proxy Language**: Saying “we” when “I” must own it.
* **Polite Deflection**: “I’m no expert, but…”
